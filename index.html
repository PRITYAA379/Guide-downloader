Nervous System Reset – Day 1

Why Nervous System Reset?

In today’s world, stress, anxiety, and overstimulation are constant triggers. Your nervous system holds the memory of every experience—both good and bad. Resetting it helps you heal, recharge, and find inner calm.

Think of your nervous system as your body’s software. Just like any software, it sometimes needs a reset to run smoothly. Through mindful breathing and meditation, you can shift from chaos to clarity.

Day 1 Practice

Step 1: Sit comfortably, spine straight, shoulders relaxed.

Step 2: Inhale deeply through the nose for 4 seconds.

Step 3: Hold your breath for 4 seconds.

Step 4: Exhale slowly through the mouth for 6 seconds.

Step 5: Pause for 2 seconds before the next breath.

Repeat for 10 minutes. Notice how your body shifts from tension to calmness.

Mind-Body Awareness

During this practice, observe your thoughts without judgment. Your goal is not to control your mind, but to gently guide it back to the rhythm of your breath. This awareness builds resilience and emotional balance.
